> **1. PLAY THE SONG**
> ```ini
> > [!play] (e.g !play Baria)
> ```
> **2. LEAVE THE CHANNEL **
> ```ini
> > [!leave]
> ```
> **3. PAUSE THE SONG**
> ```ini
> > [!pause]
> ```
> **4. SHOW QUEUE OF SONGS**
> ```ini
> > [!q | !queue]
> ```
> **5. SKIP A SONG**
> ```ini
> > [!skip]
> ```
> **6. JUMP TO THE SONG FROM QUEUE**
> ```ini
> > [!jump] (e.g !jump 3)
> ```
